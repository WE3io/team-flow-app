  ### Collection: When Git Breaks
---

The faster the tool, the smaller your pull requests should be. A huge AI-generated change is a change nobody truly reviewed — the exact way messy or unsafe code slips into `main`. Break work into small pieces, each its own PR. Small is reviewable; reviewable is safe.
