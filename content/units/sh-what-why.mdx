`main` is our single source of truth — the version that's live or ready to go live. Instead of hiding work away in long-lived side branches that drift apart and collide weeks later, everyone merges small changes back into `main` frequently. Because our AI tools let us produce changes in minutes, integrating often is what stops our work falling out of sync with each other.
