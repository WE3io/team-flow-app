Our AI tools let product and design people shape working code, not just mock-ups. But AI is fast, not careful — it can produce bloated, insecure, or subtly wrong code that looks fine. So the workflow wraps every change in cheap safety nets: small pull requests, a human glance, and automated checks. The tools give us speed; the workflow gives us safety. You need both.
