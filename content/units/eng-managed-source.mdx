  ### Collection: Non-Code & Design Work

The platform builds and deploys straight from the repo (e.g. Vercel, Netlify, Cloudflare Pages), and previews plus trunk→prod are automatic — a preview per PR, production on merge. Two consequences. Pin your build inputs for reproducibility — runtime and framework versions and build config ("framework preset" is one platform's term) — so a build is the same next month as it is today. And scan **source + dependencies** rather than an image, because there is no image artefact you control; a committed lockfile plus dependency and secret scanning is the equivalent of image scanning here.
