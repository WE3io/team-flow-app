1. **Stop.** Don't run more commands hoping to fix it.
2. **Screenshot** the message.
3. **Ping an engineer** with the screenshot and what you were doing.
Your local mess almost never affects anyone else, and branch protection stops it reaching `main`. There is no rush and no shame. Asking is the correct, professional move.
